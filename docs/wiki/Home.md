## What is FocusFlow?

FocusFlow is a productivity web application designed for individuals and teams who want to:

- **Manage tasks** with priorities, deadlines, subtasks, and categories
- **Stay focused** using the Pomodoro technique with configurable timers
- **Plan their day** with a visual time-blocking calendar
- **Leverage AI** to break down goals, schedule tasks, and identify what to tackle first
- **Collaborate** by sharing tasks, joining workspaces, and tracking team activity
